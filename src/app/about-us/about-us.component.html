About Us

The promoter of Phoenix Recruitments have been in the business of recruitment and associated services since 2001, under the name and style of Prince Enterprises. Due to difference of opinion between the Promoters, Prince Enterprises was closed in 2018, and Mr. Haleem P. decided to float Synergy Associates in 2020, with a team of highly experienced professionals in operations and marketing. The team brings with it more than 18 years of experience in the industry.
